How to Discover Your Personal Style

If you're unsure about your style, start by exploring Pinterest boards, fashion magazines, or even celebrity styles you admire. Focus on patterns in your preferences.

Basic Outfits That Always Work

Start with a capsule wardrobe: a white shirt, black jeans, a blazer, and comfy sneakers. These work for nearly any situation and help you build style confidence.

Color Combinations That Never Fail

Try beige with white, navy with grey, or pastel tones like lilac and mint green. These are safe, stylish combinations for beginners.
